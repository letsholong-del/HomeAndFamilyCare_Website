Customer confidence

What customers want to know before they book

Is the guide price final?

No. It is a starting estimate. We confirm the final quote after checking suburb, date, hours, urgency and supplies.

Can I book on WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is the fastest way to confirm the request, ask questions and receive your final quote.

Do you provide supplies?

You can provide your own supplies, or ask us to include supplies in the quote where available.

How are helpers selected?

Helpers apply with consent for reference and background checks. Final matching depends on service type, location and availability.

What if I need to cancel?

Tell us as early as possible on WhatsApp. A cancellation rule should be confirmed with your final quote before the booking is accepted.

How is my information used?

Your details are used to respond to your quote request, arrange the service and contact you about the booking. Do not send sensitive documents unless requested through a confirmed process.
