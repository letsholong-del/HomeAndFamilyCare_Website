Customer confidence

What customers want to know before they book

Is cleaning really from R300?

Yes. Once-off home cleaning starts from R300. The final quote depends on home size, duration, suburb, supplies, condition, timing and add-ons.

What does the 7-day cleaning follow-up mean?

If something from the confirmed cleaning checklist is missed, message us within 7 days so the issue can be reviewed. The final quote should confirm what was included.

Why does babysitting need a quotation?

Babysitting depends on child age, number of children, time of day, duration, transport, duties and any special family notes.

Do you provide cleaning supplies?

You can provide your own supplies, or ask us to include supplies in the quote where available.

Do you offer recurring cleaning?

Yes. Recurring cleaning can be weekly, fortnightly or monthly depending on availability in your area.

How are cleaners and babysitters selected?

Matching can include reference checks, SAPS clearance, service fit, location, availability and customer requirements.
