Customer confidence

What customers want to know before they book

Is cleaning really from R300?

Yes. Once-off home cleaning starts from R300. The final quote depends on home size, duration, suburb, supplies, timing and availability.

Why does babysitting need a quotation?

Babysitting depends on the number and age of children, time of day, duration, transport needs and duties such as meals, homework or bedtime.

Can I book on WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is the fastest way to confirm the request, ask questions and receive your final quote.

Do you only clean homes?

Yes. The current offer is home cleaning only, with once-off and recurring options. We are not positioning this site around commercial cleaning.

Do you provide cleaning supplies?

You can provide your own supplies, or ask us to include supplies in the quote where available.

How are cleaners and babysitters selected?

Helpers apply with consent for reference and background checks. Final matching depends on service type, location, suitability and availability.

What if I need to cancel?

Tell us as early as possible on WhatsApp. A cancellation rule should be confirmed with your final quote before the booking is accepted.
